Settings

Your account and what this app keeps.

Account

Signed in as —

Your data

What's kept in your account:

  • The spots you've captured, and where you were standing when you captured them
  • How far you've traveled, and the stamps you've earned
  • Guides you've made or saved
  • Gems you've submitted

Only you can read your capture history. It is never shown to another user.

Clear my data

Erases everything you've built up in Localy — your captures and the locations they were made at, your travel distance, every stamp, and every guide you've made or saved. Everywhere, not just on this phone. This cannot be undone.

Two things this does not do. Gems you've submitted stay — once approved they're public spots other people use. And guide codes you've already shared keep working for the people you sent them to, even after your copy is gone.

Your account stays open. To remove the account itself, contact support.

This permanently erases every capture, stamp and guide on your account. There is no undo and no backup.
